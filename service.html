OUR PRODUCTS

Drone Light Show

A Drone Display) is a set of multiple synchronized drones (UAVs), in most cases quadcopters, that are programmed to fly in a coordinated direction into the sky using drone software, thus creating an interesting story for public display. Hundreds of small, synchronized drones make one perfect show in the air to create an interesting, mind-blowing story that you cannot remain indifferent to.

Brand Activation

Brand activation" is an event, campaign, or any interaction through which a company drives customer actions. This technique aims to generate brand awareness, build lasting relationships/retention with the target audience, and develop customer loyalty. It helps engage more customers by enabling them to interact with the Brand directly

Street Lamp Post Advertising

Lamppost ads are ads placed on street lamp poles. It is an effective form of outdoor advertising and is currently being enjoyed by many businesses, both large and small. Lamppost ads are repetitive and easy to remember. Suitable for short and long-term campaigns

Unipole Spectacular Advertising

The Unipole is a large-format billboard type mounted atop a single steel pole. Its effectiveness is enhanced by the fact that this billboard can be seen even from long distances. The front-lit illumination adds to its versatility.

Cross Road Gantry Advertising

A "Gantry billboard" means a billboard fixed to an overhead structure, usually spanning a road (single or double carriageway). It is a double-sided structure, therefore, offering the advertiser the option of advertising to either side of the traffic.

Vehicular Bridge Panel Advertising

Advertisements are placed on a panel fixed to the side of a vehicular bridge. This highly intrusive and dominant medium of advertisement position brands right in front of approaching motorists and commuters every day, thereby making it a veritable and penetrative outdoor hoarding to reach the target audience.

Stand Alone/Portrait Advertising

Often referred to as portraits, Stand alone billboards are efficient at providing roadside coverage along inner city locations and retail precincts in high pedestrian or vehicular traffic areas

Wall Mount Advertising

Wall Mount at typically mounted on high rise building in city centres. They are usually unmissable because of their magnificent sizes which will effortlessly command audience attention.

Mobile Advertising

Bus branding is a mobile form of advertisement and a quick channel to reach the general public. Bus branding is a cost-effective way to turn the commuter bus into a professional and high-impact, mobile advertisement.
